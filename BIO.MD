# About Luong Duy Phuong
Luong Duy Phuong is an accomplished Chief Technology Officer with 15+ years of strategic expertise in network architecture, system design, and digital innovation. Currently driving technology strategy at FPT Play, he demonstrates consistent excellence in executing complex, large-scale infrastructure initiatives.

Focus Areas: Phuong specializes in HTTP streaming, digital rights management, SDN architecture, and artificial intelligence integration within enterprise system design and software development. His technical proficiency encompasses leading platforms—Cisco, Juniper, and Huawei—alongside modern programming languages and protocols (Python, YAML, DWDM), enabling him to deliver sophisticated solutions that address both technical and business objectives.

Achievements: Throughout his career, Phuong has led transformational projects including system-wide SDN implementation, carrier ethernet network upgrades (increasing national capacity to 19.2 Tbps), and comprehensive operational automation frameworks. His expertise is validated through FPT Technology Expert Level 4 certification and recognition as "Top FPT Under 35" (2017).

Goals: Phuong is committed to advancing telecommunications and OTT technology innovation, building high-performing technical teams, and developing strategic initiatives that enhance organizational competitiveness and market leadership.
